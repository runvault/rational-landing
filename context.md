# Rational — Landing Page Context

## Company

AI employees for finance & accounting — personalized to how your company actually does the work.

340,000+ accountants left the profession since 2019. 90% of firms can't find qualified staff. Companies are stuck between hiring ($70-95K loaded for a Bay Area bookkeeper) and falling behind.

Rational deploys AI employees that learn your chart of accounts, your vendor rules, your approval chains, and your exceptions. They start producing output within 48 hours of an onsite kickoff. They get better every week from your team's corrections.

YC S26 | $1K/mo pilot | $50K/yr full-time per AI employee

## Design Direction

- Minimalistic and futuristic with subtle animations
- Keep the same style as the current landing page (clean, centered, two-line hero)
- Droid Serif for logo, Montserrat for body
- #111 on white, no color noise
- No typical SaaS/AI visual tropes — no purple gradients, no emoji icons, no rounded-card-with-left-border
- Premium, serious, trustworthy tone
- Animations should be understated — entrance fades, subtle parallax, micro-interactions
- The current page is intentionally sparse — expand it but preserve the restraint
